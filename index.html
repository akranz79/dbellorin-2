About Dalila

Crafting Beauty Through Passion & Precision

With over 12 years of experience in the beauty industry, Dalila Bellorin has established herself as a premier hair stylist known for her meticulous attention to detail and artistic vision. Her journey began at the prestigious Vidal Sassoon Academy, where she honed her technical skills and developed a deep understanding of hair structure and design.

Dalila's philosophy centers around enhancing each client's unique features while maintaining the health and integrity of their hair. She believes that great hair is the foundation of confidence and self-expression.
